Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize fitness & nutrition programs for individual client needs?

Yes, you can! You have the power to tailor any program according to user goals and specific preferences while making all kind of customizations along their journey

Can I integrate existing client data into the dashboard?

You definitely can!

Is the dashboard suitable for all types of fitness goals?

We do cover a wide spectrum of muscle building and weight loss focused programs whether in a bodyweight, resistance tools or gym machines format. However, there are definitely certain fitness styles that are yet to be covered.

How can clients access their programs and log their progress?

When you add a client, you send him or her an invitation to download a standalone trainee app through which they gain access to their assigned programs

Is the platform compatible with other fitness tools and wearables?

We currently integrate with popular fitness apps like Apple Health and Google fit and are planning to roll out direct integrations with certain brands of smart watches soon!

How do I get support if I have a question?

We prioritize providing exceptional support to our valued users. If you have any questions or need assistance, our dedicated support team is here to help.
